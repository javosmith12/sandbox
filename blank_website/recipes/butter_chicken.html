Butter Chicken

Description

This amazing pasta dish is inspired directly from the caribbean! With delicious shrimp, pepper, and some spices this will surely knock you off your feet with flavor.

  • 1 lb of boneless chicken, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1/4 cup of plain yogurt
  • 1/2 cup of tomato puree
  • 1/2 cup of heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup of water
  • 1 shallot, chopped
  • 1/2 onion, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 1 tablespoon of ginger and garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoons of lemon juice
  • 2 teaspoons of garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon of chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cayenne pepper
  • 1 teaspoon of cumin
  • 1 tablespoon of cornstarch
  • 2 bay leaves
  • salt and pepper to taste
  1. On medium-high heat, add a tablespoon of oil to a pan and saute shallots and onions, about 5-6 minutes.
  2. Stir in butter, ginger and garlic pastes, lemon juice, 1 teaspoon of garam masala, chili powder, cumin, and bay leaf. Stir for a minute and add tomato sauce, cooking for another 2 minutes.
  3. In a blender, pour contents of pan, heavy cream and yogurt. Blend for 30 seconds to a minute or until preferred consistency.
  4. In a separate skillet on medium-high heat, add a tablespoon of oil and cook the boneless chicken. Cook for about 10 minutes or until chicken is lightly browned.
  5. Reduce heat to low-high on chicken and add the cayenne pepper and remaining garam masala, and a little sauce until chicken is cook to your satisfaction.
  6. Add sauce to cooked chicken. Dissolve water and cornstarch to make a slurry and add to sauce, stirring for another 5-7 minutes.
  7. Season with salt and pepper for taste. Serve and enjoy!
